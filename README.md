# 🛒 Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Data Analysis

An end-to-end Business Analytics project that transforms raw e-commerce data into actionable business insights using **Excel, MySQL, Python, and Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, payment preferences, regional sales trends, and operational efficiency.

The project follows the complete analytics workflow—from data cleaning and SQL querying to exploratory data analysis (EDA) and interactive dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw business data
- Analyze customer and sales trends
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Study regional revenue distribution
- Evaluate customer satisfaction
- Examine payment preferences
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 📍 Regional Performance

- São Paulo generated the highest revenue.
- Rio de Janeiro and Minas Gerais followed closely.
- Revenue was concentrated in a small number of states.

---

### 🛍️ Product Analysis

Top-performing product categories included:

- Bed Bath Table
- Health & Beauty
- Sports & Leisure
- Furniture & Decor
- Computers & Accessories

---

### 💳 Payment Analysis

- Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
- Boleto ranked second.
- Voucher and Debit Card had comparatively lower usage.

---

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction

- Average Review Score: **4.09 / 5**
- Most customers gave 5-star ratings.
- Customer satisfaction remained consistently high.

---

### 🚚 Operations

- Majority of orders were successfully delivered.
- Delivery times remained within an acceptable range for most customers.
- Cancelled and unavailable orders represented only a small proportion of total orders.
